Kanchipuram

Kanchipuram is an ancient city in Tamil Nadu, known as the "City of a Thousand Temples" and a major Hindu pilgrimage site, famous for its rich history as a Pallava capital and its cultural heritage. It is also a significant center for learning and is renowned for its silk sarees, which are woven using a distinctive method where the body and border are made separately
