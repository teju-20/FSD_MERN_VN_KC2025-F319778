My Top 5 Favourite Movies

  1. Inception
    • Mind-blowing storyline
    • Excellent direction by Christopher Nolan
    • Unique concept of dreams within dreams
  2. Interstellar
    • Emotional father-daughter story
    • Beautiful space visuals and science concepts
    • Outstanding background music by Hans Zimmer
  3. The Dark Knight
    • Iconic Joker performance by Heath Ledger
    • Strong storyline and action sequences
    • Intense and realistic tone
  4. Avatar
    • Stunning visual effects and world-building
    • Interesting sci-fi concept
    • Strong environmental message
  5. Spider-Man: No Way Home
    • Brings multiple universes together
    • Great emotional moments
    • Fantastic fan-service and action
